About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences. My supervisors are Prof. Tieniu Tan, Prof. Liang Wang and Prof. Yan Huang. Before that, I received the B. Eng degree from the EECS school of Peking University in 2019. My research interest includes computer vision and pattern recognition, with a focus on Embodied AI and Vision-and-Language.
