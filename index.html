About Me

I am a second-year bachelor-straight-to-PhD student in National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences. My supervisors are Prof. Tieniu Tan, Prof. Liang Wang and Prof. Yan Huang. Before that, I received the B. Eng degree from School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science in Peking University in 2019.
